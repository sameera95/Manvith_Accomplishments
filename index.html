About the Name

Manvith is a name with profound Sanskrit roots, embodying multiple meaningful interpretations:

  • Human and Humankind: The name is a direct representation of "human" or "humankind," symbolizing humanity and its essence.
  • Intellect and Wisdom: The name can be broken down from the Sanskrit words 'man' (mind or intellect) and 'vith' (knowledge or wisdom), reflecting intelligence and wisdom.
  • Positive Attributes: These meanings associate the name with positive qualities like intelligence, wisdom, and the embodiment of human virtues.
